What are Marantaceae?

Marantaceae are a family of tropical plants. Subvarieties from the genera Maranta, Stromanthe, and Calathea (alternatively classified as Goeppertia) are popular as houseplants due to the striking colors and patterns of their foliage.

They are often referred to as "prayer plants," because their leaves fold up to resemble praying hands in the evening.

Each post on this blog is dedicated to detailing the unique traits and care needs of a different variety of Marantaceae.
